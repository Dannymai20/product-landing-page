About us

Our goal at Gardena is to inspire and foster relationships through greener spaces. Our founders wanted to turn their love of gardens and natural areas into a way of life. They extend an invitation to everyone to take advantage of plants' aesthetic appeal as well as their countless health and environmental advantages. Our skilled horticulturists are here to help you create your own lush haven of peace, whether you have a green thumb or suspect you could wither a plant with a single glance.
